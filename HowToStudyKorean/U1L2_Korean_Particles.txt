!! Topic

!! Unit 2 - Lesson 2: Korean Particles 이/가

!! 있다: 2 meaning
- 있다 = to be at a location
- 있다 = to have

!! 있다: To have
- 있다 = to have | in Korean this is an adjective and cannot act on an object

- can attach ~이/가 to the object
? 나는 펜을 있다 = I have a pen (incorrect)
? 나는 펜이 있다 = I have a pen (correct)

- Indicating a Place or Time: ~에
- ~에 denotes a place or time
- possible to have ~에 used twice in a sentence

!! 있다: To be at a location | adjective
- example
? I는 school에 am at
? 나는 + 학교에 + 있다

- difference
? 나는 학교가 있다 = I have a school (this could make sense)
? 나는 학교에 있다 = I am at school
? 나는 잡지가 있다 = I have a magazine
? 나는 잡지에 있다 = I am at the magazine (this doesn’t make sense)

!! Position words
- 안 = inside
- 위 = on top
- 밑 = below
- 옆 = beside
- 뒤 = behind
- 앞 = in front

- 학교 앞에 = in front of the school

!! ~이/가 as a Subject Marker
- different
? 고양이는 집 뒤에 있다 = The cat is behind the house
? 고양이가 집 뒤에 있다 = The cat is behind the house

- 는/은 = compared with something else
